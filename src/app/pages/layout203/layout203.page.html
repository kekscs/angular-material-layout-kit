  • Key Components:  @material/top-app-bar, Sidenav
  • Behavior: In desktop mode, top app bar and drawer are always visible. Only content scrolls.
    In tablet/phone modes, drawer is hidden to maximize space available for content. It can be revealed by clicking the menu icon in top app bar. Also top app bar is not fixed anymore. Scrolling upward hides the top app bar, scrolling downward reveals the top app bar, thus freeing even more space for content.
